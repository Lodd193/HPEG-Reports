## **ğŸ” INTELLIGENT ALGORITHM**

### **Significance Thresholds:**

A trend is flagged as significant if it meets **ANY** of these criteria:

1. **High Volume CDG** (>10 cases/month) with **>15% change**
   - Example: CDG2 went from 20 to 25 cases (25% increase) â†’ Flagged

2. **Medium Volume CDG** (5-10 cases/month) with **>25% change**
   - Example: CDG8 went from 8 to 12 cases (50% increase) â†’ Flagged

3. **Large Absolute Change** (**â‰¥5 cases** regardless of percentage)
   - Example: CDG1 went from 50 to 56 cases (12% increase, but +6 cases) â†’ Flagged

4. **Dramatic Small Volume** (<5 cases/month) with **>50% change AND â‰¥3 cases**
   - Example: CDG12 went from 2 to 5 cases (150% increase, +3 cases) â†’ Flagged

**Why these thresholds?**
- Filters out noise (minor fluctuations)
- Captures meaningful trends at all volume levels
- Balances percentage changes with absolute impact
- Ensures exec team sees only actionable insights
